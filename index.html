GDG Devfest 2018

GDG DevFest Kolkata is Developer Festival that brings together the world class experts in Android, Web and Cloud technologies to the City of Joy for a day full of sessions, workshops and showcases.

The 2018 Edition will be held in University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata.

Tracks

 

android Mobile

 

Explore and Learn about the latest trends, technologies and best practices in Mobile Development. Learn from the Workshops and Hands-On sessions from experts in Kotlin, Flutter, JetPack and other latest Mobile Technologies.

 

 

cloud Cloud

 

Besides the kind that brings in rain, Computation as a whole has undergone a paradigm shift by the migration of tools and resources to the Cloud. Explore Machine Learning and AI in Cloud, Firebase, Building for the Google Assistant and a lot more from our Experts.
